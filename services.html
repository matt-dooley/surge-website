Comprehensive Powerline Services for a Sustainable Future

At Surge Powerline Services, we pride ourselves on being a full-service electrical contractor, delivering top-tier expertise and solutions across all facets of powerline and electrical infrastructure. Our dedicated team of certified professionals is committed to providing reliable, efficient, and safe electrical services, ensuring the highest standards in every project we undertake. From overhead and underground distribution to street lighting, fiber optics, emergency restoration, and renewable energy systems, we have the skills and experience to meet all your electrical needs. Partner with us for innovative solutions that power communities and drive progress.

Overhead Distribution

Our overhead distribution services encompass pole replacement, system improvements, new construction, and voltage conversion. We ensure reliable and efficient power delivery, maintaining the highest safety standards.

Storm Recovery and Emergency Restoration

Our team is ready to respond to emergencies caused by ice storms, tornadoes, hurricanes, and floods. We provide rapid restoration services to minimize downtime and restore power efficiently.

Street Lighting and Traffic Devices

We offer comprehensive installation and maintenance services for street lighting and traffic devices, enhancing public safety and infrastructure reliability in urban and rural areas alike.

Underground Distribution

We specialize in trenching underground conduits, conduit installation and maintenance, innerduct placement, and manhole installations. Our advanced techniques, including directional boring, ensure minimal disruption.

Substation & Transmission

We handle new substation construction, upgrades, and maintenance. Our team is skilled in designing, constructing, and managing complex transmission line projects, ensuring reliable power transmission.

Equipment Rentals and Leasing

We offer rental and leasing agreements with vendors, allowing us to furnish equipment for virtually any type of project. This flexibility ensures we can meet all customer requirements efficiently.

Labor Agreements and Workforce

We are signatory to labor agreements with I.B.E.W., utilizing local area linemen and electricians through referral procedures. This ensures a skilled workforce for every project.

System Analysis and Planning

Our services include analyzing and planning system maintenance and expansion, ensuring optimal performance and future growth. We prioritize system protection and maintenance upgrades for reliability and efficiency.

Line Inspection

Regular line inspections are crucial for maintaining the integrity and safety of power lines. Our thorough inspection services help prevent outages and extend the lifespan of electrical infrastructure.
